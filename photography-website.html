I'm Aryan Mehra — a Delhi-based photographer specialising in weddings, portraits, and editorial work. I believe great photography isn't about capturing what's there. It's about revealing what's felt.

My approach is patient and unobtrusive. I work with available light wherever possible, and I never impose a mood the moment doesn't already have. The result is images that feel true.
